# Business Case Understanding

# 1. What this business actually is

This is a school fundraising commerce platform.

The company enables parents to:

- buy everyday products
- choose a school
- generate donations through purchases
- invite other parents
- grow fundraising collectively

The platform connects:

- consumer brands
- parents
- schools

Shopping behavior becomes school funding.

---

# 2. How the value flows

Brands → want exposure / sales  
Parents → buy everyday products  
Platform → tracks purchases  
Schools → receive donations  

Shopping becomes fundraising.

This replaces:

- bake sales
- physical fundraising
- door-to-door selling

Buying groceries supports schools.

---

# 3. The growth engine (referral program)

Users invite parents  
New users choose school  
New users buy products  
School funding increases  

Both users receive bonuses after:

- signup
- onboarding
- linking school

This creates network-driven fundraising.

Referrals are:

- user acquisition
- fundraising amplification

---

# 4. The user persona

Target users:

- parents
- families with kids
- school communities
- PTA environments

Core user:

Parent buying groceries for kids  
Who wants to support their school  
Without extra effort

---

# 5. The main business problem

Users can:

- choose school
- browse products
- buy items
- refer friends

But users may not:

- know what to buy
- understand referral mechanics
- see impact
- stay engaged
- explore products

This creates:

- low purchases
- low referrals
- low fundraising

---

# 6. What the company wants to improve

Introduce conversational assistant to:

- help discover products
- explain referrals
- encourage participation
- increase purchases
- increase invitations

---

# 7. Why recommendations matter

Users don't search directly.

They think:

"What can I buy to support my school?"

This is a decision problem, not search.

Assistant must:

- guide decisions
- suggest products
- match preferences
- reduce friction

---

# 8. Why referral explanation matters

Referral rules:

- must use link
- must complete onboarding
- must link school
- both get bonuses

Users ask:

- When do I get rewards?
- Does friend need to buy?
- Can I invite multiple?
- Why no credit?

Assistant reduces friction.

---

# 9. Dataset confirms behavior

Data includes:

- Users
- Products
- Conversations
- Purchases (empty)

Assistant must guide before purchases exist.

---

# 10. Real business objective

Increase:

- purchases
- referrals
- engagement
- donations

AI influences behavior.

---

# 11. Assistant business role

Assistant acts as:

- shopping guide
- fundraising explainer
- referral coach
- engagement driver

---

# 12. Entire business in one sentence

Fundraising shopping platform where parents buy everyday products to support schools, and a conversational assistant increases purchases and referrals by guiding decisions.
